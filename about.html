What is WITGrades?

WITGrades is a GPA calculator that actually shows you the algorithm behind it so that you can trust it.

Who made WITGrades?

Artur Janowiec created it as a workstudy project for the Computer Science department at the Wentworth Institute of Technology (WIT). It's meant to serve all students at WIT. You can contact Artur Janowiec at janowieca@wit.edu .


In the process of creating it, an organization called WITPlusPlus was created in order to host some of the CS department's projects on github. Professor Nate Derbinsky is the owner of the organization. If you are a student who would like to work on a Computer Science project that would beneficial to the school or the CS department, please contact Nate Derbinsky at derbinskyn@wit.edu .

Have a suggestion?

If you find a bug or you have a suggestion to make the site better, send a email to Artur Janowiec or Nate Derbinsky, and one of us will handle it.
